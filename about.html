About us

Rainy Days is a clothing company created with the utmost comfort and care for every nature enthusiast out there. We started out simply wanting to make a better, yet cheaper option for people we care about and ended up supplying and supporting nature lovers all over the globe. Focusing on what matters most to us audience, we worked hard to expand and now everyone can make use of our fantastic products. With over 150 years of combined experience we took all of our knowledge and poured it into this brand creating a company used by hikers, campers and all sorts of people, all over the world.

Our vision is simplification and adaptation, helping you conquer any experience and/or challenge you set for yourself with ease. With this in mind, the idea is to make room for you to be able to enjoy your travels more, aswell as making them easier and safer. Now you can go on your hikes and trips and also look good doing it!

We have so much love for our products and are so happy that we can now share that love with all of you.
